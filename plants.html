    Plants Types

    Banyan Tree

    Banyan tree is the most familiar shade giving tree in our country. Not many people know but the banyan tree is also the national tree of India.

    Arjuna tree

    Arjuna tree is generally found around river beds.It has a woody fibrous fruit that appears between September and November.

    Peepal tree

    The peepal tree is semi- evergreen tree. This is one of those rare trees of India that release oxygen both in the day and the night.

    Neem tree

    Neem has endless medicinal properties. It is one of the best trees to plant near house in India.

    Sal Tree

    Sal is one of the largest deciduous trees in India. Interestingly, the sal tree is worshipped by Hindus and Buddhists.

    Gulmohar tree

    Gulmohar is most famous for its pretty looking flowers. In India, its flowering season is between April and June.
